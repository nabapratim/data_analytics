# 📏 Metrics Definition

## Revenue
💰 Total Revenue = SUM(quantity * price)

## Order Volume
🧾 Total Orders = COUNT(DISTINCT order_id)

## Average Order Value (AOV)
📦 AOV = Total Revenue / Total Orders

## Customer Metrics
👥
- New Customer: first-ever purchase
- Returning Customer: repeat purchaser
- Customer Lifetime Revenue = SUM(revenue by customer)

## Growth Metrics
📈
- MoM Growth = (Current Month - Previous Month) / Previous Month
- YoY Growth = (Current Year - Last Year) / Last Year

## Contribution Metrics
🧩
- % Revenue Contribution = entity revenue / total revenue
- Pareto Threshold = top contributors covering ~80% revenue
