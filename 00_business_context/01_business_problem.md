# 🧠 Business Problem

## Context
The company operates a retail sales business with multiple products and customers.
Despite consistent transaction volume, leadership observes **inconsistent revenue growth** and **unclear drivers of performance**.

## Core Problems
- 📉 Revenue growth is uneven over time
- 👥 Heavy dependency on a small % of customers
- 📦 Some products show YoY decline but reasons are unclear
- 🧩 No clarity on which segments truly matter

## Business Goals
- 💰 Identify true revenue drivers
- 🔁 Improve retention & repeat purchases
- 📊 Reduce concentration risk
- 🎯 Enable data-driven product & customer strategy

## Success Criteria
- Clear customer & product segmentation
- YoY and trend-based performance visibility
- Actionable insights, not vanity metrics
